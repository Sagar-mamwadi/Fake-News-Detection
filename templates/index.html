About the Fake News Detection System

The Fake News Detection System is designed to assist users in verifying the credibility of news articles. Using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning models, it predicts whether a piece of news is real or fake based on its content.

Our goal is to help combat misinformation and promote informed decision-making.
